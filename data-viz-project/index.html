ICE Detention in Arizona

A data-driven look at immigration detention facilities across the state

By Raphael Romero Ruiz | December 3, 2025

Eloy Detention Center holds the majority of Arizona's detained immigrant population

The vast majority of people in ICE detention in Arizona are housed in a facility in central Arizona, with six detention centers across the state holding an average of more than 4,000 individuals daily.

Source: Syracuse University's Transactional Records Access Clearinghouse (TRAC), ICE facility data as of June 23, 2025

La Paz County holds the highest average daily population of ICE detainees in Arizona

While Eloy Detention Center holds roughly one-third of Arizona's detained immigrant population, the La Paz County Adult Detention Facility near the California border has the highest average daily population in the state.

Source: Syracuse University's Transactional Records Access Clearinghouse (TRAC), ICE facility data as of June 23, 2025
